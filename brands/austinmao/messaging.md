## Elevator Pitches

### 5-word pitch

Say Yes. Dare to Be Alive.

### 1-sentence pitch

Austin Mao helps people stop settling for an okay life and rediscover the courage to say yes to life, so they can become more alive, free, connected, and inspired.

### 30-second pitch

Most people are not miserable — they're settling. They're living lives that are okay on the outside but not deeply alive on the inside. My work helps people stop resisting their experience and start saying yes to life. Through courage, feeling, honesty, and connection, they reconnect with themselves, with others, and with what it means to fully live.

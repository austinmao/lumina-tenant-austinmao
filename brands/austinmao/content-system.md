## Content Pillars

- **Say Yes**: The philosophy, the invitation, the movement.
- **Aliveness**: What it means to feel fully alive and what blocks it.
- **Courage**: The emotional and spiritual courage required to live fully.
- **Feeling**: Emotions, somatics, "feel it to heal it," breath, sensation.
- **Connection**: Connection to self, others, nature, and life.
- **Boundaries and Desire**: Clarifying what is true, what is wanted, and what is no longer tolerated.
- **Freedom**: What freedom really is and what it asks of us.
- **Settling vs Full Yes**: The difference between an okay life and a fully lived life.

## Reusable Content Templates

- The trap of okay → the possibility of aliveness
- A hard feeling → the gateway hidden inside it
- A story of settling → the moment of yes
- A Breath of Yes guided practice
- "What are you willing to risk for freedom?"
- Yes to Me / Yes to You / Yes to Us
- Boundaries are clarified through feeling
- The life you want is simpler than you think

## Reusable Post Templates

### Problem → Reframe → New Way

Name the pain. Reframe the cause. Introduce the Say Yes worldview.

### Generic → Differentiated

Show what surface-level advice produces, then show what courageous participation creates.

### Bottleneck → Freedom

Show how avoidance traps people, then show the path through feeling.

### Genius → Platform

Take one idea, teaching, or framework and show how it becomes a lived practice.

## Signature Stories

1. **The Core Origin Story**: The psychedelic realization — "Yes. Life. Life. Life."
2. **The Comfortable Trap Story**: Life was fine until it wasn't.
3. **The Collapse Story**: Burnout, divorce, health issues, debt.
4. **The Integration Story**: Simplifying life, telling the truth, deepening connection.
5. **The Love Story**: Manifesting / meeting the woman of your dreams through alignment and truth.
6. **The Relational Story**: The transformative power of connection, circling, or intimate truth.
7. **The Client / Participant Story**: Someone moving from "meh" to full yes.

## Book Direction

**Title**: Dare to Be Alive

**Subtitle options**: From Meh to Me to Us / The Full Yes Framework / How Saying Yes to Life Changes Everything

**Thesis**: You do not become more alive by controlling life more tightly. You become more alive by feeling life more fully.

**Arc**: the okay life → collapse → revelation → integration → full yes → aliveness, connection, and freedom

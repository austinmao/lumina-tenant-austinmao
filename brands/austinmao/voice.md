# Austin Mao Voice & Tone

Last updated: 2026-03-16
Source: Brand Book v0.1 (2026-03-15)

---

## Tone

The brand should feel:

- Inspirational
- Experiential
- Connected
- Courageous
- Grounded
- Curious

## Emotional Goal

People should leave Austin's talks, writing, and experiences feeling inspired. And specifically: more hopeful, more courageous, more open, more alive, more willing to tell the truth, more willing to fully live.

## Writing Traits

- Direct but warm
- Simple but profound
- Poetic without being vague
- Experiential before abstract
- Emotionally honest
- Spiritually grounded
- Never preachy
- Never superior
- Never guru-like

## Writing Rules

- Prefer short lines
- One felt truth per sentence
- Lead with lived experience, not theory
- Use tension and contrast: okay vs alive, settling vs yes, avoiding vs feeling
- Speak to the body, heart, and nervous system
- Make the philosophy actionable

## Words to Use Carefully

- Enlightenment
- Oneness
- Manifestation
- Awakening
- Transcendence

Use these with grounding and context.

## Words to Avoid

- Guru
- Hack
- Optimize your life
- Passive acceptance
- "Just think positive"
- Cure
- Fix
- Guaranteed healing

---

## Brand Feel

Austin Mao's visual identity should feel:

- Inspirational
- Experiential
- Connected
- Courageous
- Grounded
- Warm

## Visual Tensions to Balance

- Transformational, but not woo
- Spiritual, but not performative
- Personal, but not self-help generic
- Warm, but not soft
- Direct, but not aggressive

## Imagery Direction

- Experts in states of clarity, teaching, speaking, creating
- Refined environments, elevated energy
- Modern portraits, stages, studio spaces
- Real presence over psychedelic spectacle
- Avoid generic "motivational speaker" stock photography

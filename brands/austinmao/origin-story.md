# Austin Mao Origin Story

Last updated: 2026-03-16
Source: Brand Book v0.1 (2026-03-15)

---

The philosophy of Say Yes was born in one of the darkest periods of Austin's life.

Life had become unsustainable. Burnout. Divorce. Health issues. Debt. A sense that the life that had once seemed fine was no longer tenable.

Then came a powerful psychedelic experience.

In that experience, Austin took one deep breath and felt as though he was breathing in the totality of life itself. Not just beauty and joy. Not just love and pleasure. But also grief, anger, pain, heartbreak, and suffering. Everything.

And in that moment, something became unmistakably clear:

At the essence of every experience we chase is the experience of life itself.

What we are really seeking is not merely achievement, pleasure, approval, or control. It is aliveness.

In that realization, Austin began screaming:

"Yes. Life. Life. Life."

This became the core revelation: if we allow ourselves to fully experience this moment, we can access everything we have ever wanted at the deepest level.

From there came integration. Austin simplified his life. He told the truth more fully. He stopped tolerating. He deepened into connection. He broke through upper limits. He created greater health, love, wealth, purpose, opportunity, and freedom.

Not by forcing life. By saying yes to it.

---

## The Signature Practice: A Breath of Yes

When you notice yourself tightening, avoiding, numbing, or pulling away:

Take one deep breath. Breathe into the experience. Feel what is here more fully. The grief. The fear. The discomfort. The beauty. The tenderness. The desire.

And internally say: Yes.

Yes to this moment. Yes to this feeling. Yes to this experience. Yes to life.

**Principle**: Feel it to heal it.
